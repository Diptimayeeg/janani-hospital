Janani Hospital - The Best Multispecialty Hospital in Mookandapalli. Where Every Life Starts With Care and Every Story Matters..

Dr. K. Ramanuja had a mission to provide the safest and highest-quality healthcare at a reasonable price. Thus, he started a daycare centre with six to ten beds in the year 2003. Now it has grown up as the top-ranked multispecialty hospital in Mookandapalli,Hosur,Tamilnadu, only because of his hard work and dedication.

Our hospital holds the capacity of 30 in-patient bed strength, equipped with cutting-edge technology and staffed by a dedicated team of highly skilled doctors, nurses, and technicians. Apart from that, we provide 29 different kinds of expert services and have performed more than 5000 surgeries, which represents the success rate of the cases taken by our hospital.
